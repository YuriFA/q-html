  • Process

    • How does a typical project happen?
      • Introduction. First, we learn about your objectives and your property. We can evaluate your unique situation and present a development scenario.
      • Initial Agreement. Once you are comfortable with the proposal, we sign an agreement that covers the period until partnership formation and title transfer.
      • Design and Permitting. Apt invests at-risk capital to complete site surveys, design and permit the new development.
      • Partnership and Title Transfer. Once the project is fully entitled and permitted, we form a joint venture partnership to which you transfer your land.
      • Funding. Apt raises outstanding capital to develop the property.
      • Construction. Apt retains an experienced general contractor and oversees the construction of the building. Apt receives a development fee for successful project completion.
      • Operations. Apt oversees property management and distributes free cash to you, other capital partners, and a share to itself.
      • Sale. Apt coordinates the property sale after an agreed upon hold period.
